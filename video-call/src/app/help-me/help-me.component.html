How to Start Video Calling

1. Open Your Camera: Click the "Open Your Camera" button to turn on your camera and microphone.

2. Copy Your Peer ID: Use the "Copy Invite Code" button to copy your Peer ID to the clipboard.

3. Call a Friend: Enter your friend's Peer ID in the text box and click "Call".

4. End Call: Click "End" to terminate the video call.

5. Share Screen: Click "Share Screen" to share your screen with your friend.
